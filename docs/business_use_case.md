# Business Use Case

## Scenario

A small analytics software company sells products and services across four US regions. Leadership wants to understand which parts of the business are performing well, where margin or cost pressure exists, and what decision should be made next.

The company tracks:

- Monthly revenue
- Profit
- Gross margin
- Marketing spend
- Operating cost
- Units sold
- Region
- Product category
- Customer segment
- Retention and churn risk

## Target Users

This app is designed for:

- Business analysts
- Student analysts building public project evidence
- Small business operators
- Product or growth teams
- Internship interview reviewers evaluating analytics ability

## Decisions The App Helps With

### Growth Allocation

The app identifies strong regions and categories so a team can decide where to increase marketing or sales focus.

### Profitability Management

The app compares revenue growth with profit growth. If profit weakens faster than revenue, the app flags cost control as a decision area.

### Product Mix Review

The app identifies categories with high unit volume but weak margin. This helps leadership decide whether to adjust pricing, reduce cost, or shift focus.

### Retention Focus

The app uses retention and churn risk to identify when customer retention should be prioritized before acquisition spending.

### Scenario Planning

The app lets a user test assumptions before making a decision. For example:

- What if marketing spend increases by 15%?
- What if prices increase by 5%?
- What if operating costs rise by 10%?
- What if demand falls by 8%?

## Business Value

Retail KPI & Forecasting Sandbox demonstrates how analytics can move beyond reporting. It connects metrics to decisions, explains the logic behind recommendations, and gives users a structured way to test business assumptions.
